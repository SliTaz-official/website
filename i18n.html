SliTaz i18n project

Internationalization

SliTaz project Website is available in the following languages:

Deutsch - English - Español - Français - - Português

The main goal of SliTaz is to provide full support for a given language, including the website, official documentation, Wiki community and a dedicated forum.

The website and documentation has been translated and is kept up-to-date by several contributors. For now, the actively maintained languages are English, French, German and Portuguese. Spanish is on the stove, but more help is needed. Most of the communication is done through the Mailing List and the Wiki.

Some additional languages can be installed through the Package Manager, but we are not able to provide support and documentation. SliTaz is a community based project, everyone can help out and is more than welcome to join us.
