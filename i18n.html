SliTaz i18n project

Internationalisation

SliTaz i18n project have the mission to translate SliTaz. The main goal of SliTaz is to provide full support for a given language, including website, official documentation, Wiki community and a dedicated forum.

The website and documentation have been translated and are kept up-to-date by several contributors. For now, the actively maintained languages are English and French. German and Spanish are on the stove, but help is needed. Most of the comunications are done on the Mailing List and the Wiki.

Some additinnals languages can be installed throught the Packages Manager, but we are not able to provide support and documentation. SliTaz is a community based project, everyone can help and is more than welcome to join us.
